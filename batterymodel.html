Battery model

We use multimodel approach and the battery is made of sub-models also called components.

class Battery(paramobj)

Bases: PhysicalModel

The battery model consists of

  • an Electrolyte model given in Electrolyte property

  • a Negative Electrode Model given in NegativeElectrode property

  • a Positive Electrode Model given in PositiveElectrode property

  • a Thermal model given in ThermalModel property
